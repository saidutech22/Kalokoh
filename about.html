About Us

Codeskul is a technology training center dedicated to empowering individuals with essential computer and digital skills for today's world. Founded by Mr. Alie Kalokoh, Codeskul focuses on providing practical, beginner-friendly learning in area such as computer basics, Microsoft Office, web development, and coding.

Our mission is to bridge the digital gap by equipping students, youths, professionals with the knowledge and confidence to thrive in a tech-driven society. Through hands-on training, real-world projects, and supportive learning environment, Codeskul is shaping the next generation of digital leaders.

At Codeskul, we believe that technology should be accessible to all and we're committed to making that a reality.
